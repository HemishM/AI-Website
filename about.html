The story behind CollabrAI.

The Problem

Every group project starts the same way.

Two people show up, one person disappears, and somehow everyone gets the same grade. We've all been there — staying up late finishing work that was supposed to be split evenly, with no way to prove it. That frustration is exactly what CollabrAI was built to fix.

The Solution

While you grind, they sleep.

One teammate checks out and the rest of the group picks up the slack. CollabrAI detects inactivity early — before it becomes your problem. Automatic alerts flag when someone falls behind so the team can redistribute tasks before the deadline becomes a crisis.

What's Next

Data that actually tells the story.

CollabrAI turns raw document activity into a clear, visual breakdown of who did what. No more guessing, no more confrontation — just objective data your whole team can see. Starting with students, the same transparency is coming to every team in every company that works in groups.
